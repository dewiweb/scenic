[DESCRIPTION]
Scenic is software suite for transmission and reception of your fine audio-visual content. It is an interface to the milhouse command-line Gstreamer-based software.

[SYNOPSIS]
scenic [options]
.TP
Most options are set with the graphical user interface.

[STATE SAVING]
The configuration is saved in the user's ~/.scenic directory, where "~" is the path to your home directory. 

[EXAMPLES]
Here is how to start scenic in the kiosk mode: 
.TP
scenic --kiosk
