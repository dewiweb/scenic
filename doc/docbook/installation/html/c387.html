Chapter 3. Hardware Setup

Scenic require the use of many devices. In this chapter, we will have an overview of what kind of device are generaly use. We will also give some idea on how to connect these devices. What problem the you could face and how to troubleshoot them.

3.1. Generic connection sheme

The diagram below illustrate a generic Scenic connection sheme:

Audio Input:

  • Microphone in mic entry (or other audio inputs)

  • Sound card output channels 1 and 2 in mixer input lines 7 and 8

Audio Output:

  • Subgroup 1 – send to sound card input channels 1 and 2

  • Subgroup 2 – send to speakers

  • Headphones

  • External sound card connected to USB (optional setup)

Video Input:

  • Video signal from camera to S Video input on video capture card NOTE: If composite cable, insert yellow connecter.

Video Output:

  • DVI to projector / VGA to monitor
